Our Courses

Tamale Islamic Senior High School (TISSEC) offers three core programs approved by the Ghana Education Service (GES). Each program includes specific elective combinations that allow students to tailor their studies to their interests and career goals.

1. General Science

Students in General Science may choose from the following elective combinations:

  • Science and Mathematics
  • Science with Computer Science
  • Science with Economics
  • Science with Agricultural Science
  • Science with Geography

These combinations prepare students for careers in engineering, health sciences, data & technology, economics, environmental science, and more.

2. Home Economics

Home Economics is offered in two main options, each with specific subject pairings:

• Food and Nutrition Option

  • Food and Nutrition with Biology
  • Food and Nutrition with Chemistry

• Textiles and Clothing Option

  • Textiles and Clothing with Biology
  • Textiles and Clothing with Chemistry

These combinations support careers in nutrition, food technology, hospitality, fashion design, textile engineering, and health sciences.

3. Agricultural Science

Students in Agricultural Science may choose from the following combinations:

  • Agricultural Science with Elective Mathematics
  • Agricultural Science with Computer Science

These pairings prepare students for modern agribusiness, farm management, agricultural engineering, data-driven farming, and agri-tech innovation.

All students also take core subjects including English, Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies, as required by the GES curriculum.
